Frequently Asked Questions

What happens in a fit call?

We conduct a 90-minute conversation about your business challenges, growth goals, and current situation. We'll explore whether there's mutual alignment and if we can help you achieve your objectives.

Is there any cost for the fit call?

The initial fit conversation is complimentary. Our goal is to understand your situation and determine if there's mutual alignment for a partnership.

What size businesses do you work with?

We typically work with SMBs generating $1M-$50M in annual revenue. These businesses have proven product-market fit but need operational structure to scale effectively.

How quickly can we get started?

Most fit calls are scheduled within 1-2 weeks. If there's good alignment, we can typically begin our engagement within 30 days.
